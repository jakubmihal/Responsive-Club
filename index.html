About us

We are great discord community of Kicom's youtube Bitcoin Channel. We communicate in our czech native language, we discuss and teach others about bitcoin, security, investing, liberty and others interesting topics.

Clubhouse thursdays

We meet every thursday at 8:30PM on clubhouse app, where we discuss everything about bitcoin.

Cryptopub saturdays

We meet every saturday at 9:00PM on discord. It started during the coroma pandemic, when all pubs where shutted down, that why we call it cryptopub. We discuss there everything important that is happening in the world.

Youtube sundays

We meet every sunday at 8:00PM on Kicom's youtube Bitcoin Channel, where he's streaming on any crypto related topics.
